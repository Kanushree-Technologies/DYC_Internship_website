Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Send us a message through the contact form or visit our office with basic identification. Membership is open to all youth aged 13-25 years.

Is there a membership fee?

We offer affordable membership options. Contact our office for current membership plans and rates.

How often are activities held?

Activities are held regularly throughout the week. Different programs have different schedules. Check activities page for details.

Can I volunteer?

Yes! We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds. Fill the volunteer form or contact us for more information.

How can I register for events?

You can register for events through our contact form or by visiting our office. Registration details are provided in event announcements.

Are your programs free?

Most programs are free for members. Some special workshops and events may have minimal fees to cover materials and speakers.
